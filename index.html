Paula Carrascal

I am an audiovisual artist and performer exploring programming languages looking to enhance my art through digital mediums. As an artist, my practice is surrounded by digital images and sound which I interpole with programming languages in order to obtain new results from them. I like to think of my work as a poetic insight from the world that combines itself with the digital realm, abstracting a new vision of it.
